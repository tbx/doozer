Mozilla India Pune Demoparty

June 25th 2011

What

Join Mozilla India for a full day + night of open web (aka HTML5) demo hacking, food/drinks! An artful exploration of open web technologies, in the tradition of the Demo Scene. Attend hands-on HTML5 workshops and collaborate with hackers and artists till late night or even the next day to take home the evenings Demo Party Award.

Who

HTML5 and Open Web geeks, DemoSceners and Internet Artists The India Pune Demoparty is organized by Mozilla India and Mozilla Labs as part of DemoParty, an online competition and series of activities to foster artful development and exploration of web technologies.

Details
