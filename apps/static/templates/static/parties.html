The Flame Party Helsinki

June 18th, 2011

Join us in Helsinki for a day of HOT Open Web (aka HTML5) demo hacking, outdoor BBQ, beers and Finnish saunas! Attend hands-on HTML5 workshops during the day and collaborate with fellow hackers/artists till late night to take home the Flame Party Award!! Organized by the Alternative Party Crew, DOT and Mozilla Labs.

DemoJS Paris

July 1st - July 2nd, 2011

DemoJS Party is a french demo party focussed only on web technologies (JS, Canvas, SVG, Audio... well everything in the web). This event is brought to you by demoscene.fr, the Javscript user group Paris, Prologin and Mozilla Labs.

Solskogen Oslo

15th-17th of July, 2011

We're partnering with the Solskogen.no demoparty in Oslo, Norway! Join us for 2 days of web (aka HTML5) demo hacking, soccer, camping, Amigas, outdoor BBQ, beers and Scandinavian hospitality! Like at “The Flame Party” in Helsinki, Solskogen 2011 will also challenge you to participate in a “Single Effect Compo“. Full compos can be submitted to the combined demo category also.
